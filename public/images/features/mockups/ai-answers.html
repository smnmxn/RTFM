How do I invite someone to my team?

AI-generated answer

To invite a team member to your project, follow these steps:

  1. Go to your Project Settings and click the Team tab
  2. Click the "Invite member" button
  3. Enter their email address and select a role: Editor (can draft), Publisher (can approve), or Admin (full access)
  4. They'll receive an email with an invitation link that expires in 7 days

Once they accept, they'll have access to the project based on the role you assigned.
