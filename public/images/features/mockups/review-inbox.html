How to invite team members
Getting Started
Learn how to invite colleagues to your project and manage their permissions. Team members can help review articles, manage your help centre, and collaborate on content.
Step 1
Open Team Settings
Navigate to your project settings and click the "Team" tab in the sidebar.
UI mockup
Step 2
Send an invitation
Click "Invite member", enter their email address, and choose a role (Editor, Publisher, or Admin).
Step 3
Track invitation status
Pending invitations appear at the bottom of the team list. Links expire after 7 days.
